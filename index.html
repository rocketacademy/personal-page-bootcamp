From Architect to Software Engineer

ARCHITECTURE X SOFTWARE

Being in the construction industry as an architect for 4 years and managing several projects from conception to construction and completion, I experienced the primitive nature of the industry in many aspects. As inevitable as it seems, this backward nature has manifested in a lack of framework and systems, resulting in many holes and inefficiencies across stakeholders in projects.

I realised the endless potential and possibilities coding and programming bring to the table, and that these holes can be improved by introducing programs and interfaces, but there are few within the industry equipped with the necessary knowledge. This drove me to delve into software engineering and coding, with an aspiration of being able to better and transform the construction industry in the future.
