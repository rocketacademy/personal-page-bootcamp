My Github About Me

Welcome to my portfolio page!

I first got hooked on product management when I had the chance to intern at Xiaomi China during my undergraduate days. There, I saw how a company was built around its products, where product managers played integral roles in bringing both business and technical priorities together. I knew I wanted to be at the centre of action, diving deep into what makes products tick from both business and technical perspectives, and working across stakeholders to bring ideas to life, imagine new ways to delight customers and be at the forefront of growing businesses. After my first product development stint with DBS, I’m now a Product Manager with YouTrip, where I manage full-cycle product development for B2C product verticals, from user validation and prototyping, to shipping into production and the eventual launch. I’m proudest of having spearheaded product development in 2 key markets (Singapore and Thailand), achieving 3x transaction volume increase, projected 2x growth in user base, as well as a new authentication system for users in Thailand. It’s been an exciting ride, full of growth and learnings, and I won’t have it any other way! Outside of work, I’m all about building and developing products too. I co-founded SAGE, an online knowledge-sharing platform for >500 youths to connect with industry mentors. I’ve also started Goodhub SEA, a community for youths to learn & build Tech for Good across Southeast Asia I just love building new things and bringing visions to life. As good product management requires cross-functional knowledge and experience, I’m all about immersing myself and building across industries (B2B and B2C), domains (finance, tech and more) and borders (Singapore, Southeast Asia and China) so that I can contribute effectively.
